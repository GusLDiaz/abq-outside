Use Case

Attitudes and Needs: Health minded, wants something to do after classes (preferably something outdoors)

Why choose this site over other options: It's simple and not complicated for someone with basic computer skills to understand and navigate. Doesn't need to put to much personal information when signing up or posting comments/reviews.

Usage Preconditions: Wants to find a site where he can find something to do that can keep him active and outdoors during his free time and also share his experinces with other people.

Usage Postconditions: Will be able to find trails closest to him for a quick hike/walk and share his experiences with other people through the site

Frequency of Use: 1-3 times a month (expected frequency of use). Maybe he gets addicted; uses it 4 times a week?

User Story

Kyle researches for a site where he can find outdoor activities to do during his freetime after his classes and on the weekends and finds a site that he can easily navigate and is perfect for him because he can find trails that are close to him

Interaction Flow

#1

  1. Navigates to ABQ Outside via Laptop
  2. Looks for a trail on map page/ Homepage
  3. Finds a trail closest to him
  4. Clicks on trail
  5. Displays some information on trail within a sidebar
  6. Clicks anywhere within sidebar to display new page (on same tab) with more trail info and comments/reviews
  7. Looks through top comments/reviews
  8. Decides he wants to go to this trail so he clicks on a "Get me there" button where he is sent to a google maps version of the map where he can use GPS.

#2

  1. Goes back on site after having an amazing experience on first trail.
  2. Decides he wants to share his experience and leave a rating
  3. Makes a profile
  4. Clicks first trail he went to and sidebar comes up
  5. Clicks on sidebar and is taken to new page with more info on the trail with comments and reviews
  6. Goes down to comments/reviews and finds comment box.
  7. Puts in a comment describing his overall experience and a picture that he took during the hike and puts a thumbs up review for the trail.
  8. Submits comment
  9. Decides he wants to use the site again for future use so he bookmarks page
  10. He also decides he want to go to the trail again in the future so he adds the trail to a favorites by using a "Your Trails" button
